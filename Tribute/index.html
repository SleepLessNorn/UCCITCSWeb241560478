Tribute to Iron Man

Tony Stark – The Man Behind the Armor

Tony Stark, also known as Iron Man, is a genius billionaire, inventor, and philanthropist. Using his intellect and resources, he built a high-tech suit of armor to save lives and fight for justice. As a founding member of the Avengers, his courage and innovation helped protect the world from countless threats.

Legacy

From the first Iron Man suit built in a cave to the ultimate sacrifice in Avengers: Endgame, Tony Stark inspired millions with his bravery, humor, and heart. His journey reminds us that anyone can be a hero — even without superpowers.
